## OKX Margin Trading: Cross Margin Aur Isolated Margin — Antar Kya Hai?

Yeh sabse important technical sawaal hai jo har beginner ko confusion me daalta hai. OKX me do main margin modes hain, aur dono ka risk profile alag hai.

### Cross Margin Mode

Cross margin mode me aap ke saare trading account ke assets ek shared pool me jaate hain. Iska matlab hai ki agar aap ka ek position loss me hai, toh doosre positions aur assets bhi us loss ko cover karne ke liye use ho jaate hain. Yeh mode capital efficiency zyada deta hai kyunki aap ka poora balance margin ka kaam karta hai.

OKX ki documentation ke anusaar: "In multi-currency cross-margin mode, you can trade all instruments, including spot or spot with margin, expiry futures, perpetual futures, and options."

Cross margin ke do sub-types hain:

- **Spot and futures cross margin**: All assets and liabilities under positions displayed hote hain. Margin base ya quote currency dono me ho sakta hai.

- **Multi-currency/Portfolio margin**: Aap borrow karke crypto kharid sakte ho ya borrowed crypto transfer kar sakte ho (auto-borrow enabled hone par). Liabilities cross-margin assets ke neeche dikhte hain aur free margin USD value me calculate hota hai.

### Isolated Margin Mode

Isolated margin me aap har position ke liye alag se collateral rakh sakte ho. Agar ek position liquidate ho jaata hai, toh doosre positions aur aap ka baaki balance safe rehte hain. Yeh risk management ke liye zyada behtar hai.

OKX me isolated margin ke do modes hain:

- **Auto transfers**: Jab aap position open karte ho, toh required margin automatically cross-margin account se isolated account me transfer ho jaata hai. Base ya quote currency dono margin ke liye use ho sakte hain.

- **Quick margin**: Aap manually collateral assets transfer karte ho, phir borrow karte ho. Iske baad trading spot trading jaisa lagta hai.

### Cross Vs Isolated: Konsa Choose Karein?

| Feature | Cross Margin | Isolated Margin |

| --- | --- | --- |

| Risk sharing | Saare positions shared pool me | Har position alag isolated |

| Capital efficiency | Zyada (saara balance margin) | Kam (sirf allocated amount) |

| Liquidation risk | Ek position doosre ko drag kare sakta hai | Sirf wahi position affected |

| Best for | Experienced traders, hedging | Beginners, risk management |

| Margin flexibility | Base/quote dono currencies | Base/quote dono currencies |

Beginners ke liye isolated margin zyada recommended hai. Agar aap ek hi trade par focus karna chahte ho aur baaki balance safe rakhna chahte ho, toh isolated mode lo. Experienced traders jo hedging strategies use karte hain, wo cross margin me zyada flexibility paate hain.

## OKX Margin Trading Me Risk Management: Liquidation Se Kaise Bachein?

Margin trading ka sabse bada risk liquidation hai. Jab aap ka margin level 100% ya usse kam ho jaata hai, toh platform forcefully aap ki position sell kar deta hai. OKX ka system tiered liquidation use karta hai — yani positions gradually reduce hote hain, ekdum se sab nahi.

### Liquidation Warnings

OKX ki help documentation ke anusaar:

- **300% margin level**: Aap ko liquidation warning milta hai.

- **100% margin level ya kam**: Partial ya full liquidation trigger hota hai.

Iska matlab hai ki jab aap ka margin level 300% ke aas-paas aaye, toh alert ka signal hai. Neeche aane se pehle position close kar lo ya additional margin add karo.

### Risk Management Tips

1. **Leverage kam rakho**: 10x leverage tempting hai, lekin 2x-3x se shuru karo. Profit kam lagega, lekin loss bhi kam hoga.

2. **Stop-loss use karo**: Har trade me stop-loss lagao. OKX me order panel me stop-loss functionality available hai.

3. **Position size small rakho**: Apne total capital ka 5-10% se zyada ek single margin trade me mat lagao.

4. **Isolated mode se shuru karo**: Beginners ke liye isolated margin zyada safe hai kyunki ek position loss doosre positions ko affect nahi karta.

5. **Margin level monitor karo**: Regularly apne margin level check karo. 300% ke neeche aate hi alert ho jao.

6. **Interest timing ka fayda uthao**: OKX me interest hourly calculate hota hai. Agar aap 1 ghante se kam ke liye trade karte ho aur sahi time par repay karte ho, toh interest avoid kar sakte ho.

> "Margin trading me loss se bachna impossible hai, lekin unhe minimize karna possible hai. Liquidation ka dar healthily rakho — yeh aap ko disciplined banata hai. Lekin dar ka alawa risk management tools bhi use karo."

## OKX Margin Trading vs Futures Trading: Konsa Behtar Hai?

Yeh ek common confusion hai. Dono me leverage use hota hai, lekin structure kaafi alag hai.

### Margin Trading

- Aap actual crypto borrow karte ho aur real assets trade karte ho.

- Leverage maximum 10x tak.

- Interest hourly calculate hota hai.

- Long aur short dono kar sakte ho.

- Position size borrowing limits par depend karta hai.

### Futures Trading

- Aap contracts trade karte ho, actual crypto nahi.

- Leverage up to 100x tak available.

- Funding rates periodic hote hain (perpetual futures me).

- Contracts settle expiry par ya cash-settled hote hain.

- Position size contract specifications par depend karta hai.

### Kab Konsa Use Karein?

- **Margin trading**: Jab aap ko actual crypto exposure chahiye aur moderate leverage kaafi hai. Misal ke taur par, agar aap BTC ko long karna chahte ho aur position hold karna chahte ho, toh margin trading sahi hai.

- **Futures trading**: Jab aap ko zyada leverage chahiye ya short-term price movement se fayda uthana hai. Day traders futures prefer karte hain.

> "Margin trading aur futures trading me ek bada antar yeh hai ki margin me aap real crypto borrow karte ho, jabki futures me contracts settle hote hain. Tax aur accounting ke hisaab se yeh farq matter karta hai."
